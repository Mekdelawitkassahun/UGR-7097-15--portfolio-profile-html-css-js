About

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Mekdelawit Kassahun, a full stack web developer, freelancer, and entrepreneur dedicated to crafting engaging digital experiences. I specialize in building dynamic web applications that blend creativity and technology to solve real problems. My journey spans web design, UX/UI, and web development. I believe great design is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new photography projects or working on entrepreneurial ventures. I’m always eager to learn and collaborate, so feel free to reach out to discuss potential projects! At my school, a home to many orphans, I’ve gained invaluable life lessons. Their resilience inspired me to cultivate a strong work ethic, not only to create a successful future for myself but also to contribute to the well-being of others.
